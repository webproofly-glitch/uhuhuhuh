Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Coinlarp, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • License Key – Your unique license key for app activation
  • Device Information – Device ID and type for license binding
  • Email Address – For account-related communications

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • IP address (for security and analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Activate and manage your license
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Storage and Security

Your personal data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data transmission (HTTPS)

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the services you requested
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we're required by law to retain certain data.

5. Third-Party Services

Coinlarp may use third-party services for:

  • Payment Processing – SellAuth for license purchases
  • Analytics – To understand app usage patterns
  • Customer Support – Telegram for support communications

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction – Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion – Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability – Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection – Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@coinlarp.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Coinlarp is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Coinlarp after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Cookies

Coinlarp uses minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analyzing app performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain features of the app.
