Welcome to Coinlarp

Your license is active. Follow these steps to install Coinlarp as a standalone app on your device.

Installation Steps

1
Open this page in Safari.
This will not work from Chrome or other browsers on iOS.
2
Tap the Share Button.
Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari's toolbar.
3
Add to Home Screen.
Tap "Add to Home Screen" in the share sheet.
4
Tap Add.
Coinlarp will now appear on your home screen.

After adding, tap the Coinlarp icon on your home screen to open the app.
