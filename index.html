FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coinlarp?
Coinlarp is a 1-1 crypto wallet that lets you simulate crypto and stock portfolios with custom balances and tokens. Flex your hypothetical gains without risking real money.
Is Coinlarp free to use?
No, Coinlarp requires a license to use. You can try our 7-day Starter plan or choose our Pro or Lifetime plans for full access.
Can I use real money with Coinlarp?
No, Coinlarp is a simulation tool only. You track hypothetical portfolios with custom balances - no real money is involved. Perfect for testing strategies!
How do I get updates?
All plans include free lifetime updates. You'll get new features and improvements automatically through the app.
Which devices are supported?
Coinlarp is available on both iOS and Android.
